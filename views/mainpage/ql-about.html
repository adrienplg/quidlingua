English-French and French-English Translation Solutions

For the legal and Human Resources communities

Quid Lingua is a French-English and English-French translation service provided by Audrey Pouligny, a legal professional with an in-depth knowledge of both civil law and common law.

Audrey is a French and American dual citizen. She holds Master’s Degrees in private civil law, international employment and labor law, as well as in common law, comparative law and global mobility, from the Universities of Panthéon-Assas Paris II, Lumière Lyon II, and Descartes Paris V.

She is licensed to practice law in France and has extensive litigation experience in both France and the U.S., spanning multiple practice areas. She has also counseled international clients on a wide array of issues concerning employment law and Human Resources.

Audrey is a member of the Northern California Translators Association (NCTA). She prides herself on offering intelligent translations based on genuine legal and HR know-how.
